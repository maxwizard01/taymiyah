Raising Knowledgeable & God-Fearing Students

At Ibnu Taemiyyah Model College, our mission is to raise knowledgeable, disciplined, and God-fearing students who excel academically and uphold strong moral values. We are committed to delivering quality education that combines sound academic instruction with Islamic teachings. From Junior Secondary School (JSS1) to Senior Secondary School (SS3), we provide a supportive and conducive learning environment where students are guided to develop intellectually, spiritually, and socially. Our holistic approach prepares students for higher education and responsible living in society.

Why Choose Our School

Online Result Portal

Our Online Result Portal provides a secure and convenient platform where students and parents can access academic results anytime and from anywhere. With just a few clicks, parents can monitor their child’s academic performance, track progress across terms, and stay informed without the need to visit the school physically. The portal is user-friendly, reliable, and designed to ensure confidentiality and accuracy of student records.

Qualified Teachers

At Ibnu Taemiyyah Model College, we pride ourselves on having a team of highly qualified, experienced, and passionate teachers. Our educators are professionally trained and dedicated to nurturing students academically, morally, and socially. Through effective teaching methods, continuous assessment, and personal mentoring, our teachers are committed to helping every student reach their full potential..

Islamic & Moral Training

Islamic and moral education is at the heart of our institution. We place strong emphasis on character development, discipline, respect, and Islamic values in line with the Qur’an and Sunnah. Our students are guided to develop good manners, honesty, responsibility, and a strong sense of faith, preparing them to become morally upright individuals and responsible members of society.
